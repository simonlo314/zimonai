Pricing FAQ

Questions about plans.

What's included in the free assessment?
The free assessment includes the full questionnaire and a preview of your risk score and key risk factors. To receive the detailed PDF report with recommendations, you'll need to purchase a plan.
Can I upgrade from Essential to Deep Diagnostic?
Yes. If you've already purchased an Essential report and want to upgrade, contact us at lab@zimonai.com and we'll apply the credit toward a Deep Diagnostic.
What does the money-back guarantee cover?
If you're not satisfied with your report within 30 days of purchase, we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.
Is the Elite Hardware Advisor right for my company?
The Elite plan is designed for IT teams managing 10+ devices, companies with strict hardware procurement policies, or professionals with high-value device ecosystems. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
Do you offer volume discounts?
Yes. For teams purchasing 5+ reports, we offer custom enterprise pricing. Reach out at lab@zimonai.com or visit our contact page.
